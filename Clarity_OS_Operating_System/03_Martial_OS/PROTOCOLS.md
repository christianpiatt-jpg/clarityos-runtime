# Martial OS Protocols 
Protocols are structured responses to pressure, conflict, or destabilization. 
They ensure the operator does not collapse, overreact, or lose clarity. 
 
## Core Protocols 
1. Anchor Protocol 
   - Re-establish physical, emotional, and cognitive footing. 
   - Prevents drift and panic. 
 
2. Boundary Protocol 
   - Identify and assert the minimum boundary needed for stability. 
   - Prevents collapse into someone else's frame. 
 
3. Pressure Reading Protocol 
   - Determine direction, intensity, and source of pressure. 
   - Prevents misdiagnosis and escalation. 
 
4. De-escalation Protocol 
   - Reduce intensity without surrendering agency. 
   - Creates space for ULDA to run cleanly. 
 
5. Stabilization Protocol 
   - Restore a workable geometry after disruption. 
   - Prepares the environment for Executive action. 
 
## Why protocols matter 
Protocols protect the ULDA loop from collapse under pressure. 
They ensure the operator stays inside their own geometry and does not get hijacked.
