# Global OS Diagnostics 
Global Diagnostics provide tools for assessing planetary-scale stability and drift. 
They help operators understand whether global systems are strengthening, weakening, or transforming. 
 
## Diagnostic categories 
- Stability Indicators: coherence across major fields. 
- Drift Indicators: misalignment between systems and constraints. 
- Pressure Indicators: rising tension across boundaries. 
- Propagation Indicators: speed and direction of global spread. 
 
## Global failure modes 
- Field Collapse: a major global field loses coherence. 
- Cascade Failure: instability spreads across regions. 
- Boundary Breach: constraints fail and pressure spills over. 
- Overload: systems cannot process incoming pressure. 
 
## Outputs 
- Global stability maps. 
- Drift forecasts. 
- Intervention recommendations. 
- Cross-regional propagation models.
