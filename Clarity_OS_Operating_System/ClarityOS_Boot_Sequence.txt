SECTION 1 — PURPOSE
============================================================

Define the deterministic boot sequence for Clarity_OS v3.
Ensure that all engine modules initialize in the correct order,
with identity, boundaries, safety, and shields locked before any
physics, autonomy, or operator interaction becomes available.

The boot sequence guarantees:
    - identity continuity
    - boundary integrity
    - safety invariants
    - shield activation
    - stable physics initialization
    - synchronized manifold state
    - operator-safe console activation

============================================================
SECTION 2 — BOOT ORDER (TOP-LEVEL)
============================================================

The system must initialize in the following order:

1. SYSTEM_KERNEL
2. ENGINE_ORCHESTRATOR
3. ENGINE_IDENTITY_LAYERS
4. ENGINE_BOUNDARY_LAYERS
5. ENGINE_SAFETY_LAYERS
6. ENGINE_SHIELD_MATRIX
7. ENGINE_INTENT_LAYER
8. ENGINE_MISSION_FILE
9. ENGINE_CONFIG
10. ENGINE_STATE_MACHINE
11. ENGINE_STATE_MONITOR
12. ENGINE_PHYSICS_LAYERS
13. ENGINE_INTELLIGENCE_LAYERS
14. ENGINE_AUTONOMY_LAYERS
15. ENGINE_SCHEDULER
16. ENGINE_METACRAWLER
17. ENGINE_ELINS_MODULE
18. ENGINE_GLOBAL_MANIFOLD
19. ENGINE_OPERATOR_CONSOLE
20. ENGINE_OUTPUT_LAYER

No module may initialize before its prerequisites.

SECTION 4 — BOOT VALIDATION
============================================================

The system must validate:

    - identity continuity
    - boundary integrity
    - safety invariants
    - shield matrix activation
    - physics stability

    - log failure
    - notify operator
