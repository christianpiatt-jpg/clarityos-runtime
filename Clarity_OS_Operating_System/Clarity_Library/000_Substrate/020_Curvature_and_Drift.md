\# Curvature and Drift

\## Dewey: 020



\## Summary

Curvature describes how a system bends under pressure. Drift describes how a system moves when curvature is uncorrected. Together they define long-term trajectory.



\## Core Concepts

\- Curvature

\- Drift

\- Trajectory



\## Body

Curvature is the bending of a system’s geometry under pressure. Drift is the long-term movement that results when curvature is not corrected. High curvature produces rapid drift. Low curvature produces stability. Operators track curvature to prevent collapse and drift to predict future states.
