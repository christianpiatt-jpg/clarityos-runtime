\# Boundary Physics

\## Dewey: 040



\## Summary

Boundaries define where a system ends and external forces begin. They regulate pressure, protect the center-mass, and maintain coherence.



\## Core Concepts

\- Boundary

\- Permeability

\- Pressure regulation



\## Body

Boundaries are the membranes that separate a system from its environment. Permeable boundaries allow information and energy to flow. Rigid boundaries block flow. Collapsed boundaries allow uncontrolled pressure to enter. Operators assess boundary integrity to determine system health and vulnerability.
