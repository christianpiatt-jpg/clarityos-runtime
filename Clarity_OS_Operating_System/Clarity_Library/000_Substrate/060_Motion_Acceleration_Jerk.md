\# Motion, Acceleration, Jerk

\## Dewey: 060



\## Summary

Motion is change. Acceleration is change of change. Jerk is the sudden spike in acceleration that destabilizes systems.



\## Core Concepts

\- Motion

\- Acceleration

\- Jerk



\## Body

Motion describes how a system changes over time. Acceleration describes how quickly that change increases. Jerk is the sudden spike in acceleration that destabilizes systems and triggers collapse. Operators track jerk to detect early warning signs of breakdown.
