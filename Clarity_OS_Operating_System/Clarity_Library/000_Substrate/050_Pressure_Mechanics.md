\# Pressure Mechanics

\## Dewey: 050



\## Summary

Pressure is the force acting on a system. It creates curvature, triggers drift, and initiates collapse sequences when unmanaged.



\## Core Concepts

\- Pressure

\- Load distribution

\- Collapse triggers



\## Body

Pressure is any force—emotional, institutional, relational—that acts on a system. Pressure creates curvature. Sustained pressure creates drift. Excessive pressure triggers collapse. Operators must identify the source, magnitude, and distribution of pressure to stabilize the system.
