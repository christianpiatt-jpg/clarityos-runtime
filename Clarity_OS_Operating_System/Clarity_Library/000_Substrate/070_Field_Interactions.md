\# Field Interactions

\## Dewey: 070



\## Summary

Systems interact through fields—emotional, institutional, narrative. These fields transmit pressure and shape behavior.



\## Core Concepts

\- Fields

\- Interaction

\- Transmission



\## Body

Fields are invisible structures that transmit pressure between systems. Emotional fields transmit affect. Institutional fields transmit norms. Narrative fields transmit meaning. Operators map field interactions to understand how pressure propagates across environments.
