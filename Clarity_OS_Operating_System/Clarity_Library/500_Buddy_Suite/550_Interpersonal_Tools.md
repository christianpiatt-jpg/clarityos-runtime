\# Buddy Field Dynamics

\## Dewey: 550



\## Summary

Buddy field dynamics describe how emotional and narrative fields behave within Buddy interactions. They explain how presence, tone, and pacing affect stability.



\## Core Concepts

\- Field sensitivity

\- Presence and pacing

\- Emotional load distribution



\## Body

Buddy interactions create a shared field. Field dynamics describe how pressure moves within that field: how tone, pacing, and presence either stabilize or destabilize the interaction. Buddies learn to sense when the field is tightening, fragmenting, or softening, and adjust their moves accordingly. This connects interpersonal work directly to emotional physics and room mechanics.
