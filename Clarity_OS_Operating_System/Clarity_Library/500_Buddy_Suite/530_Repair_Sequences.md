\# Repair Sequences

\## Dewey: 530



\## Summary

Repair sequences are structured paths for restoring connection after rupture, misunderstanding, or collapse. They guide Buddies and operators through stepwise interpersonal repair.



\## Core Concepts

\- Rupture identification

\- Stepwise repair

\- Re-stabilization



\## Body

Repair sequences provide a clear path from rupture back to stability. They begin by naming what happened, acknowledging impact, and re-establishing safety. Then they move through clarification, responsibility, and forward agreements. Repair sequences are designed to be pressure-aware and basin-sensitive, ensuring that repair does not re-trigger collapse.
