\# Buddy Primitives

\## Dewey: 510



\## Summary

Buddy primitives are the smallest building blocks of Buddy interactions: acknowledgments, reflections, clarifications, and boundary statements. They are the atoms of interpersonal clarity.



\## Core Concepts

\- Acknowledgment

\- Reflection

\- Boundary statement



\## Body

Buddy primitives are simple, repeatable moves that can be combined into more complex scripts. Acknowledgment names what is happening without judgment. Reflection mirrors back experience in clean language. Boundary statements define what is safe, possible, or not available. Operators and Buddies use these primitives to build stable, pressure-safe interactions.
