\# Buddy Microscripts

\## Dewey: 520



\## Summary

Buddy microscripts are short, reusable interaction patterns for high-frequency situations: overwhelm, shutdown, confusion, and conflict. They provide ready-to-use language that is mechanically safe.



\## Core Concepts

\- Script patterns

\- High-frequency scenarios

\- Safety-first phrasing



\## Body

Buddy microscripts are pre-designed phrases and sequences that can be used in common high-pressure moments. Each microscript is built from Buddy primitives and tested for mechanical safety: it reduces pressure, preserves agency, and avoids escalation. Microscripts are not rigid; they are templates that can be adapted to basin, context, and relationship.
