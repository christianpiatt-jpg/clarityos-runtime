\# Drift Maps

\## Dewey: 320



\## Summary

Drift maps show how institutions move over time under sustained pressure. Drift is predictable and reveals long-term failure trajectories.



\## Core Concepts

\- Drift vector

\- Long-term trajectory

\- Pressure-induced motion



\## Body

Institutions drift when pressure is uncorrected. Drift maps show the direction and magnitude of this motion. Operators use drift maps to predict institutional failure, identify early warning signs, and design interventions that restore stability.
