\# Attractor Dynamics

\## Dewey: 340



\## Summary

Institutions fall into attractor states—stable, unstable, or collapsing. Attractor dynamics determine how institutions respond to pressure and whether they recover or fail.



\## Core Concepts

\- Stable attractor

\- Unstable attractor

\- Collapse attractor



\## Body

Attractors are the long-term states institutions settle into. Stable attractors absorb pressure. Unstable attractors amplify it. Collapse attractors accelerate drift and failure. Operators identify attractor states to determine whether intervention is possible or whether collapse is inevitable.
