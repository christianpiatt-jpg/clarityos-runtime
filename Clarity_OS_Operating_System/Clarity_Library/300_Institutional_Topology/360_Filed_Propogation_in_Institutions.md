\# Field Propagation in Institutions

\## Dewey: 360



\## Summary

Fields—emotional, narrative, institutional—propagate through organizations in predictable patterns. Field propagation determines how pressure spreads.



\## Core Concepts

\- Field transmission

\- Propagation vectors

\- Institutional permeability



\## Body

Fields move through institutions based on shape, permeability, and pressure gradients. Emotional fields propagate through relationships. Narrative fields propagate through communication channels. Institutional fields propagate through policy and culture. Operators map propagation to predict how pressure will spread.
