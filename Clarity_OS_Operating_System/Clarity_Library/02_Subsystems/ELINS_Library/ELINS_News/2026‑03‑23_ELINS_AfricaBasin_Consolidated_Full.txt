# **E — ENERGY / ECONOMY**

### **Public Signal**
- Africa faces a **looming energy drought**: decades of under‑investment + Hormuz instability + depleted reserves.  
- Oil shock shrinks **Eid celebrations** continent‑wide — household austerity as macro indicator.  
- Airlines (Ethiopian, SAA, Air Sénégal) hit by fuel shock; fares rising.  
- Africa’s **$155bn borrowing forecast** threatened by Middle East war fallout.  
- “Hidden debt” scandals erupt (Senegal, Mozambique, Zambia) → fiscal earthquakes.  
- 2005’s debt cancellation + mobile revolution still shape today’s economic architecture.

### **Internal Interpretation**
Africa is in a **structural energy‑fiscal squeeze**:

- Energy scarcity → inflation → subsidy strain → austerity.  
- Hidden debt → opacity premium → higher sovereign borrowing costs.  
- Airlines + logistics → immediate operational stress.  
- Households → first‑order victims of macro shocks.

### **Trajectory (7/14/21)**
- **7:** More subsidy interventions; airlines adjust routes.  
- **14:** Debt‑transparency demands intensify from lenders.  
- **21:** Energy rationing and price controls spread.

### **Archetype**
**The Under‑Fueled Continent** — scarcity + opacity define the economic field.
