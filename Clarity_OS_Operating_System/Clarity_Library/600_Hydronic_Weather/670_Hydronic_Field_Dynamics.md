\# Hydronic Field Dynamics

\## Dewey: 670



\## Summary

Hydronic field dynamics describe how atmospheric fields interact with emotional physics, institutional topology, and basin shape.



\## Core Concepts

\- Field interactions

\- Atmospheric coupling

\- Multi-layer dynamics



\## Body

Hydronic fields interact with emotional physics, institutional topology, and basin shape. Field dynamics describe how pressure moves across layers, how fronts form, and how anomalies propagate. Operators use field dynamics to understand multi-layer interactions and anticipate systemic shifts.
