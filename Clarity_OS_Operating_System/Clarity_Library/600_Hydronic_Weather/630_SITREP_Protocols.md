\# SITREP Protocol

\## Dewey: 630



\## Summary

SITREP Protocol provides a standardized method for reporting atmospheric conditions: pressure, drift, fronts, anomalies, and stability.



\## Core Concepts

\- SITREP format

\- Atmospheric indicators

\- Stability assessment



\## Body

SITREPs are structured atmospheric reports that summarize hydronic conditions in a basin, institution, or global region. They include pressure levels, drift vectors, fronts, anomalies, and stability assessments. SITREPs allow operators to track motion over time and coordinate interventions across basins.
