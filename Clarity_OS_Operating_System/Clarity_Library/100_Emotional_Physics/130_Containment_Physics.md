\# Containment Physics

\## Dewey: 130



\## Summary

Containment physics describes how emotional load is held, buffered, or released within a system. Proper containment prevents collapse and stabilizes motion.



\## Core Concepts

\- Containment vessel

\- Load buffering

\- Controlled release



\## Body

Containment is the ability of a system to hold emotional load without leaking or collapsing. Strong containment allows for processing and metabolization. Weak containment leads to leakage, projection, or collapse. Operators strengthen containment by reinforcing boundaries and reducing gradients.
