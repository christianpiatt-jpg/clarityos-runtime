\# Energy Rings

\## Dewey: 100



\## Summary

Energy rings describe the layered structure of emotional charge around a person or system. Each ring has different stability, permeability, and reactivity.



\## Core Concepts

\- Inner ring (identity)

\- Middle ring (meaning)

\- Outer ring (behavior)



\## Body

Energy rings form the emotional geometry of a system. The inner ring holds identity and is the least permeable. The middle ring holds meaning and is moderately permeable. The outer ring holds behavior and is the most permeable. Pressure applied to inner rings produces greater distortion and collapse risk.
