\# Field Loading

\## Dewey: 140



\## Summary

Field loading describes how emotional fields accumulate pressure over time. Loaded fields distort perception and accelerate collapse.



\## Core Concepts

\- Field saturation

\- Load accumulation

\- Distortion threshold



\## Body

Fields accumulate pressure from repeated interactions, unresolved conflict, or institutional drift. As load increases, the field becomes saturated and distorts perception. Operators measure field loading to determine when intervention is required and how to dissipate accumulated pressure.
