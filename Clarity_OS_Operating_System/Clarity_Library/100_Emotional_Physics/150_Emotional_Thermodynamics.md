\# Emotional Thermodynamics

\## Dewey: 150



\## Summary

Emotional thermodynamics describes how emotional energy moves, transforms, and dissipates. It governs the conservation and flow of emotional load.



\## Core Concepts

\- Energy transfer

\- Dissipation

\- Conservation



\## Body

Emotional energy follows thermodynamic principles. It transfers between systems, dissipates through expression or metabolization, and accumulates when blocked. Operators use thermodynamic principles to guide stabilization, dissipation, and recovery.
