\# Narrative Canon

\## Dewey: 800



\## Summary

The Narrative Canon defines how ClarityOS communicates with the public: tone, structure, safety, and narrative mechanics. It ensures that external communication is stable, clear, and emotionally ergonomic.

The Narrative Canon establishes the principles for all public-facing communication. It defines how clarity is transmitted through essays, tools, briefings, and diagnostics. It ensures that public artifacts are mechanically sound, emotionally safe, and aligned with the deeper layers of ClarityOS.
