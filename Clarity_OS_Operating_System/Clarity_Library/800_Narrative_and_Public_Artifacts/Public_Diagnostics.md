\# Public Diagnostics

\## Dewey: 840



\## Summary

Public diagnostics translate hydronic weather, basin dynamics, and institutional topology into accessible, non-technical briefings for general audiences.



\## Core Concepts

\- Public-facing diagnostics

\- Accessible atmospheric reporting

\- Narrative translation



\## Body

Public diagnostics provide simplified interpretations of hydronic weather, basin dynamics, and institutional motion. They translate complex atmospheric data into accessible briefings that help the public understand large-scale motion without overwhelming detail.
