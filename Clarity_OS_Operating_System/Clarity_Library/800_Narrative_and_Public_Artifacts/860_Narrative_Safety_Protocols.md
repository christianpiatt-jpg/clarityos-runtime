\# Narrative Safety Protocols

\## Dewey: 860



\## Summary

Narrative safety protocols ensure that public communication does not trigger collapse, overwhelm, or destabilization. They define safe boundaries for public clarity.



\## Core Concepts

\- Narrative safety

\- Emotional boundaries

\- Public stability



\## Body

Narrative safety protocols protect audiences from destabilizing content. They define what can be said publicly, how it should be framed, and what boundaries must be maintained. These protocols ensure that public clarity is stabilizing, not overwhelming.
