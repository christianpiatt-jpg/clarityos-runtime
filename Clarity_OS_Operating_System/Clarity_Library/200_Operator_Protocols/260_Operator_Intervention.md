\# Operator Intervention

\## Dewey: 260



\## Summary

Operator intervention is the active application of tools, diagnostics, and stabilization techniques to restore coherence and prevent collapse.



\## Core Concepts

\- Intervention timing

\- Load management

\- Boundary reinforcement



\## Body

Operator intervention is the moment when diagnostics become action. Interventions are chosen based on ELINS output, pressure gradients, and collapse maps. Effective intervention requires timing, precision, and awareness of system stability. Poorly timed intervention increases pressure and accelerates collapse.
