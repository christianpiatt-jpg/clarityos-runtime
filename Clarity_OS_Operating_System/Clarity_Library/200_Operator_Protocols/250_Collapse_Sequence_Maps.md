\# Collapse Sequence Maps

\## Dewey: 250



\## Summary

Collapse sequence maps show the predictable progression of system failure. They allow operators to identify collapse early and intervene before acceleration spikes.



\## Core Concepts

\- Collapse progression

\- Early warning signals

\- Intervention points



\## Body

Collapse follows a predictable sequence: curvature → drift → acceleration → jerk. Collapse maps show how this sequence unfolds in different environments. Operators use these maps to identify early warning signals and intervene before collapse becomes irreversible.
