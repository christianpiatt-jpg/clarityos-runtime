\# Planetary Diagnostics

\## Dewey: 940



\## Summary

Planetary diagnostics analyze global hydronic conditions, cross-basin interactions, and systemic stability across the entire mesh.



\## Core Concepts

\- Global stability

\- Cross-basin analysis

\- Systemic indicators



\## Body

Planetary diagnostics provide a global view of hydronic conditions. They analyze cross-basin interactions, global arcs, and systemic stability. Operators use planetary diagnostics to anticipate global turning points and coordinate interventions across the mesh.
