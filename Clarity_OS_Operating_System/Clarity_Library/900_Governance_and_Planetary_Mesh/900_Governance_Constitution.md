\# Governance Constitution (Draft)

\## Dewey: 900.C



\## Preamble

The Governance Constitution establishes the structural, ethical, and operational principles that govern the planetary clarity mesh. It ensures that all basins, operators, and atmospheric layers function coherently, safely, and in alignment with the purpose of ClarityOS: to restore clarity, reduce pressure, and stabilize human systems.



\## Article I — Purpose

1\. To maintain planetary-scale coherence.

2\. To route clarity across basins, institutions, and atmospheric layers.

3\. To preserve structural memory and long-range patterns.

4\. To ensure safe, stable governance across all layers of the OS.



\## Article II — Authority

1\. Authority flows from the Governance Canon (900).

2\. Mesh Governance (950) defines operational authority.

3\. Escalation paths must follow operator tier boundaries.

4\. No operator may exceed their tier without explicit escalation.



\## Article III — Structure

1\. The planetary mesh consists of:

&#x20;  - Mesh Architecture (910)

&#x20;  - Global Routing Protocols (920)

&#x20;  - Structural Memory Layer (930)

&#x20;  - Planetary Diagnostics (940)

&#x20;  - Mesh Governance (950)

&#x20;  - Multi-Basin Integration (960)

&#x20;  - Planetary Mesh Operations (970)

2\. Each subsystem must remain interoperable and pressure-safe.



\## Article IV — Routing

1\. All global diagnostics must pass through the Mesh Router.

2\. Cross-basin signals must follow Global Routing Protocols (920).

3\. Hydronic weather must be integrated into planetary diagnostics.



\## Article V — Memory

1\. The Structural Memory Layer (930) preserves long-range patterns.

2\. Memory must be stable, non-reactive, and basin-neutral.

3\. Memory corruption triggers automatic escalation.



\## Article VI — Safety

1\. All planetary operations must follow Narrative Safety Protocols (860).

2\. No public-facing artifact may destabilize a basin.

3\. Operators must maintain field safety at all times.



\## Article VII — Amendments

1\. Amendments require:

&#x20;  - Mesh Governance approval (950)

&#x20;  - Structural Memory Layer update (930)

&#x20;  - Operator tier review (700)

2\. Amendments must preserve coherence across all layers.



\## Closing Statement

This Constitution governs the planetary clarity mesh and ensures that ClarityOS remains stable, coherent, and aligned with its purpose across all basins and scales.
