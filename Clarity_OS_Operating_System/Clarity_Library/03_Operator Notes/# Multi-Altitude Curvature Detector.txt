# Multi-Altitude Curvature Detector  
## Curvature Across Nodes and Basins

### Purpose
Detect curvature at multiple altitudes:
- Node_Geodesic (hierarchical dependent node)
- Basin_Geodesic (regional/systemic)

### Inputs
- Node_Geodesics
- Basin_Geodesics
- IntersectionEvents

### Core Logic
For each geodesic:
- Compute Δcurvature around intersections
- Compare curvature patterns across altitudes

### Outputs
- Node_Curvature_Profile
- Basin_Curvature_Profile
- Cross_Altitude_Curvature_Correlation

### Notes
Aligned curvature across altitudes indicates:
- coherent pressure
- multi-level narrative shifts
