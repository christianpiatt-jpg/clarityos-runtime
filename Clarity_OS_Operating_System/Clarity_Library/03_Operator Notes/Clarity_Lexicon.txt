CLARITY LEXICON v1.0 — PRIMITIVES

1. Boundary  
   Definition: The edge of responsibility.  
   Function: Reduces emotional load; prevents over‑identification.  
   Kernel Mapping: Responsibility Router.

2. Pattern  
   Definition: Structural repetition independent of personality.  
   Function: Depersonalizes conflict; reveals system loops.  
   Kernel Mapping: Structural Scanner.

3. Pacing  
   Definition: The nervous system’s tempo relative to the situation.  
   Function: Slows escalation; restores temporal clarity.  
   Kernel Mapping: Nervous System Regulator.

4. Meaning vs Manifest  
   Definition: The gap between intent and what actually appears.  
   Function: Detects drift; prevents misinterpretation loops.  
   Kernel Mapping: Alignment Detector.

5. Tripwire  
   Definition: The moment accountability activates.  
   Function: Clarifies when responsibility becomes unavoidable.  
   Kernel Mapping: Accountability Trigger.

6. Neutral Clarity  
   Definition: Clarity as a mechanical property, not a moral stance.  
   Function: Removes emotional charge; keeps conversations safe.  
   Kernel Mapping: Emotional Load Dampener.

7. Room Motion  
   Definition: The collective direction of pressure in a group.  
   Function: Predicts escalation vectors; guides navigation.  
   Kernel Mapping: Collective Pressure Mapper.
