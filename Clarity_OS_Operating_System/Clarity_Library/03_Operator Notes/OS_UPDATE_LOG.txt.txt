A basin is a cultural or linguistic environment with its own emotional physics. Basins shape how people interpret pressure, meaning, and narrative. Operators use the basin canon to understand how interventions must be adapted across environments. Basins are not stereotypes; they are mechanical descriptions of how meaning behaves in different contexts.
