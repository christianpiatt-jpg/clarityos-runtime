# QC ENGINE — OPERATOR NOTES
Version: v2
Layer: Interpretation → Stabilization

------------------------------------------------------------
## PURPOSE
The QC_ENGINE evaluates the force‑picture produced by upstream modules and
stabilizes interpretation before it reaches identity or action layers.

QC stands for:
- Quality of Clarity
- Quieting Charge
- Quantized Calibration

Its core function:
→ Assign probabilities, thresholds, and force‑weights WITHOUT narrative.

QC is the “cooling layer” of ClarityOS v2.

------------------------------------------------------------
## INPUTS
- ER_TRANSLATOR.output (force‑picture, relational geometry)
- Environmental context (optional)
- System load (optional)

------------------------------------------------------------
## OUTPUTS
- probabilities  
- possibilities  
- thresholds  
- force_weights  
- stabilized interpretive frame  

These outputs feed directly into:
- IDENTITY_LAYER_V2  
- AGENCY_ENGINE_V2  
- MARKOV_QC  

------------------------------------------------------------
## OPERATOR POSTURE
When using this module, the operator maintains:

- neutrality  
- slow evaluation  
- non‑threatening posture  
- curiosity about likelihoods  
- separation of signal from story  

The operator asks:
“What is *likely* happening based on the force‑picture?”

Not:
“What does this *mean about me*?”

------------------------------------------------------------
## FAILURE MODES
QC becomes distorted when:

- emotional charge is high  
- the operator wants a specific outcome  
- identity is upstream of interpretation  
- the operator collapses probabilities into certainties  
- the system is overloaded  

Symptoms include:
- overconfidence  
- premature conclusions  
- threat inflation  
- identity wobble  
- agency misfires  

------------------------------------------------------------
## RECOVERY PROTOCOL
If QC degrades:

1. Pause interpretation.  
2. Return to ER_TRANSLATOR’s force‑picture.  
3. Remove all personal meaning.  
4. Re‑evaluate only probabilities and thresholds.  
5. Confirm neutrality before passing to identity.  

This restores interpretive stability.
