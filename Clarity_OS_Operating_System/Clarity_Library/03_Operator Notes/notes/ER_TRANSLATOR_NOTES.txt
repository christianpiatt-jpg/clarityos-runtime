------------------------------------------------------------
MODULE: ER_TRANSLATOR (v2)
LAYER: Energy → Meaning Bridge
POSITION IN SPINE: [3] Between Patterns and QC
------------------------------------------------------------

PURPOSE
Translate relational patterns into energetic signatures.
This module answers:
“What is the energetic meaning of this pattern?”

It does NOT:
- assign motive
- assign morality
- assign identity
- jump to QC

It simply maps:
pattern → energy → signal

------------------------------------------------------------
INPUTS
- pattern signatures from PATTERNED_SHAPES
- relational geometry
- force vectors
- environmental context
- historical echoes

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUTS
- energetic signature
- charge level
- direction of energetic flow
- activation state
- pre-QC signal

These outputs feed directly into:
- QC_ENGINE
- IDENTITY_LAYER_V2

------------------------------------------------------------
OPERATOR POSTURE
Stay neutral.
Stay descriptive.
Stay upstream of judgment.
Stay inside the energy, not the story.

Operator questions:
“What is the energy doing?”
“How is it moving?”
“What is the charge?”

------------------------------------------------------------
FAILURE MODES
- moralizing the energy
- personalizing the signal
- collapsing energy into motive
- skipping straight to QC
- confusing energy with identity

Symptoms:
- emotional contamination
- premature judgment
- distorted charge readings

------------------------------------------------------------
RECOVERY PROTOCOL
1. Return to pattern geometry.
2. Identify only the energetic motion.
3. Remove all motive-language.
4. Re-map the energy flow.
5. Re-run the translation.
