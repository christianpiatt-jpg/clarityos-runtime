# AGENCY ENGINE v2 — OPERATOR NOTES
Version: v2
Layer: Action Selection → Bounded Agency

------------------------------------------------------------
## PURPOSE
AGENCY_ENGINE_V2 determines what actions are available to the operator
based on a stabilized identity and a neutral interpretive frame.

Its core function:
→ Generate clean, bounded, ethical options without emotional distortion.

This module ensures that agency is:
- downstream of identity
- constrained by ethics and boundaries
- nonreactive
- structurally aligned with the situation

It is the “decision geometry” of ClarityOS v2.

------------------------------------------------------------
## INPUTS
- IDENTITY_LAYER_V2.output (clear-self, stable reference frame)
- QC_ENGINE.output (probabilities, thresholds, force-weights)
- Environmental constraints (optional)
- Ethical constraints (required)

------------------------------------------------------------
## OUTPUTS
- options  
- clean_yes  
- clean_no  
- wait_states  
- bounded action geometry  

These outputs feed into:
- 04_Executive_Layer (v1)
- 05_Meta_Executive (oversight)
- MARKOV_QC (temporal weighting)

------------------------------------------------------------
## OPERATOR POSTURE
When using this module, the operator maintains:

- calm agency  
- nonreactive decision posture  
- clarity about boundaries  
- patience with timing  
- willingness to choose “wait”  

The operator asks:
“What are the *structurally clean* options available to me?”

Not:
“What do I feel like doing?”

------------------------------------------------------------
## FAILURE MODES
Agency becomes distorted when:

- identity is unstable  
- QC is bypassed  
- emotional charge is high  
- the operator collapses into urgency  
- boundaries are unclear  

Symptoms include:
- impulsive action  
- overcommitment  
- avoidance  
- false urgency  
- collapsing options into binaries  

------------------------------------------------------------
## RECOVERY PROTOCOL
If agency degrades:

1. Return to IDENTITY_LAYER_V2.  
2. Confirm the reference frame is stable.  
3. Re-run QC to check probabilities and thresholds.  
4. Remove emotional urgency.  
5. Re-generate options from a neutral posture.  

This restores clean agency.
