------------------------------------------------------------
MODULE: GEOMETRIC_INTERPRETER (v2)
LAYER: Perception → Raw Geometry Extraction
POSITION IN SPINE: [1] Upstream Anchor
------------------------------------------------------------

PURPOSE
Extract raw geometric forms from any situation before interpretation.
This module answers only one question:
“What is the shape of what is happening?”

------------------------------------------------------------
INPUTS
- sensory data
- relational layout
- spatial arrangement
- motion vectors
- boundary positions
- force directions

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUTS
- geometric form (triangle, line, cluster, orbit, collapse, expansion)
- boundary map
- force‑vector map
- spatial relationships
- raw structure without meaning

------------------------------------------------------------
OPERATOR POSTURE
Describe, do not interpret.
Stay upstream.
Stay literal.
Stay structural.

------------------------------------------------------------
OPERATOR CHECKLIST
[ ] Identify the geometric form.
[ ] Identify boundaries.
[ ] Identify force direction.
[ ] Identify motion.
[ ] Identify spatial relationships.
[ ] Do NOT interpret meaning.
[ ] Do NOT assign motive.
[ ] Do NOT personalize.

------------------------------------------------------------
FAILURE MODES
- jumping to interpretation
- assuming motive
- collapsing geometry into emotion
- skipping straight to QC or identity

------------------------------------------------------------
RECOVERY PROTOCOL
1. Stop.
2. Return to geometry.
3. Describe only what is physically present.
4. Re‑extract shapes and vectors.
