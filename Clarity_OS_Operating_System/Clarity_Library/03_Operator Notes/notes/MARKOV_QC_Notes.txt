# MARKOV QC — OPERATOR NOTES
Version: v2
Layer: Temporal Weighting → Drift & Arc Stability

------------------------------------------------------------
## PURPOSE
MARKOV_QC applies temporal weighting to the QC outputs and evaluates how
the system is likely to evolve over time.

Its core function:
→ Update probabilities, detect drift, and stabilize the operator’s sense of arc.

This module ensures:
- no overreaction to single moments
- no collapse into short-term turbulence
- no runaway narratives
- no false certainty about the future

It is the “time geometry” engine of ClarityOS v2.

------------------------------------------------------------
## INPUTS
- QC_ENGINE.output (probabilities, thresholds, force-weights)
- PENTAGON_SPHERE_GEOMETRY.output (system shape)
- GLOBAL_ARC_REPORT (macro-scale signals)
- TOPOLOGY_STATUS (system stability indicators)

------------------------------------------------------------
## OUTPUTS
- updated_weights  
- arc_probabilities  
- drift_alerts  
- stability_signals  
- temporal smoothing  

These outputs feed into:
- Executive layers (v1)
- Meta-Executive (oversight)
- Identity & Agency (indirectly)

------------------------------------------------------------
## OPERATOR POSTURE
When using this module, the operator maintains:

- long-horizon awareness  
- patience with uncertainty  
- comfort with partial information  
- nonreactivity to short-term spikes  
- structural calm  

The operator asks:
“What is the *trajectory* of this situation?”

Not:
“What does this moment mean?”

------------------------------------------------------------
## FAILURE MODES
Temporal reasoning becomes distorted when:

- the operator overweights the present moment  
- emotional charge collapses the timeline  
- the operator assumes certainty  
- drift signals are ignored  
- the system is overloaded  

Symptoms include:
- false urgency  
- timeline collapse  
- overprediction  
- misreading arcs  
- identity wobble  

------------------------------------------------------------
## RECOVERY PROTOCOL
If temporal clarity degrades:

1. Pause all predictions.  
2. Return to QC probabilities.  
3. Re-anchor in PENTAGON_SPHERE_GEOMETRY (macro shape).  
4. Re-run temporal weighting slowly.  
5. Confirm drift signals before updating arc probabilities.  

This restores temporal stability.
