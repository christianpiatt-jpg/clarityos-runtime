BOUNDARY
  Where your responsibility ends.
  → Restores agency • Lowers emotional load.

PATTERN
  What repeats independent of personality.
  → Depersonalizes conflict • Reveals structure.

PACING
  Tempo of the moment vs tempo of the body.
  → Slows escalation • Returns control.

MEANING vs MANIFEST
  Intent vs what actually appears.
  → Detects drift • Prevents misread loops.

TRIPWIRE
  The moment accountability activates.
  → Clarifies action • Stops avoidance cycles.

NEUTRAL CLARITY
  Clarity as a mechanical property.
  → Removes moral charge • Keeps the room safe.

ROOM MOTION
  The direction the group is moving.
  → Predicts escalation • Guides navigation.
