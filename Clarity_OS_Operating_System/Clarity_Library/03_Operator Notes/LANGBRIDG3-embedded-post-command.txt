#3_SINGLE_CONCEPT_UNIT(concept):

Beat 1 — CONFLICT (Turn 1)
    State the tension in one clean sentence.

Beat 2 — VECTOR-COLLISION (Turn 2)
    Name the two forces hitting each other (no geometry terms).

Beat 3 — IMPACT (Turn 3)
    Describe the effect on the person/system in one sentence.

Beat 4 — RETURN (Turn 3 continuation)
    Offer the stabilizing move or reframe in one sentence.

Rules:
    - One concept only.
    - Four sentences total.
    - No dome terms.
    - No stance/resistance language.
    - No multi-layer explanations.
    - No teaching.
    - No abstractions.
    - Route pressure, don’t explain it.
