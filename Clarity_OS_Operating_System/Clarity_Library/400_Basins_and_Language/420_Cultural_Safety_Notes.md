\# Cultural Safety Notes

\## Dewey: 420



\## Summary

Cultural safety notes identify the pressure points, sensitivities, and narrative hazards within a basin. They protect operators from accidental destabilization.



\## Core Concepts

\- Cultural pressure points

\- Narrative hazards

\- Safety protocols



\## Body

Every basin has cultural sensitivities that amplify pressure or distort meaning. Cultural safety notes identify these hazards and provide operators with guidelines for safe engagement. They prevent accidental escalation and ensure interventions land cleanly.
