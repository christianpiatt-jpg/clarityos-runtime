\# Basin Distortion Maps

\## Dewey: 450



\## Summary

Basin distortion maps show how meaning bends under cultural pressure. They reveal predictable distortions that occur in different linguistic environments.



\## Core Concepts

\- Meaning distortion

\- Cultural curvature

\- Narrative drift



\## Body

Meaning bends differently in each basin. Some basins amplify emotional content. Others suppress it. Some basins distort hierarchy; others distort vulnerability. Basin distortion maps show how meaning will bend under pressure, allowing operators to adjust their interventions accordingly.
