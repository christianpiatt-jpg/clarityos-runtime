\# Field Safety Training

\## Dewey: 750



\## Summary

Field safety training prepares operators to work in live environments without causing harm to themselves or others. It emphasizes stability, boundaries, and escalation awareness.



\## Core Concepts

\- Field safety

\- Boundary management

\- Escalation awareness



\## Body

Field safety training teaches operators how to maintain stability in live environments. It covers boundary management, pressure containment, escalation awareness, and collapse prevention. Operators learn how to recognize when a situation exceeds their tier and how to hand off safely.
