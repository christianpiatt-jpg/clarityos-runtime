\# Drills and Practices

\## Dewey: 720



\## Summary

Drills and practices are structured exercises that build operator skill, stability, and reflexes. They translate theory into embodied capability.



\## Core Concepts

\- Skill drills

\- Reflex training

\- Embodied practice



\## Body

Drills and practices provide operators with repeatable exercises that build clarity reflexes. These include pressure-mapping drills, ELINS walkthroughs, basin-shift simulations, and hydronic weather interpretation. Drills are designed to be short, repeatable, and progressively challenging.
