\# Escalation Authority

\## Dewey: 760



\## Summary

Escalation authority defines when and how operators escalate situations to higher tiers, institutional channels, or hydronic-level diagnostics.



\## Core Concepts

\- Escalation thresholds

\- Authority boundaries

\- Multi-layer escalation



\## Body

Escalation authority ensures that operators know when a situation exceeds their training or tier. It defines escalation thresholds, authority boundaries, and multi-layer escalation paths. Proper escalation protects the operator, the system, and the field.
