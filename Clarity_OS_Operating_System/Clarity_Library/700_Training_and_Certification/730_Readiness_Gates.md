\# Readiness Gates

\## Dewey: 730



\## Summary

Readiness gates are checkpoints that determine whether an operator is prepared to advance to the next tier or take on new responsibilities.



\## Core Concepts

\- Competency thresholds

\- Gate criteria

\- Advancement readiness



\## Body

Readiness gates ensure that operators advance only when they have demonstrated stable capability. Gates include competency assessments, scenario walkthroughs, and field-safety evaluations. They protect both the operator and the systems they serve by preventing premature escalation.
