\# Training Canon

\## Dewey: 710



\## Summary

The Training Canon defines the principles, structure, and pedagogy of ClarityOS training. It ensures consistency, safety, and upward integration across all operator development.



\## Core Concepts

\- Pedagogical structure

\- Layered learning

\- Safety-first training



\## Body

The Training Canon outlines how operators learn ClarityOS: from emotional physics to hydronic weather, from interpersonal tools to institutional topology. Training is layered, cumulative, and safety-first. Each module builds on the previous one, ensuring operators develop stable, integrated capability.
