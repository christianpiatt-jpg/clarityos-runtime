\# Operator Tiers

\## Dewey: 700



\## Summary

Operator tiers define the levels of capability, authority, and responsibility within ClarityOS. Each tier corresponds to specific skills, tools, and field permissions.



\## Core Concepts

\- Tier progression

\- Capability thresholds

\- Field permissions



\## Body

Operator tiers provide a structured path for developing clarity practitioners. Each tier represents a distinct level of capability, from basic emotional physics literacy to full multi-basin, multi-layer operational authority. Tiers ensure that operators only engage with systems they are trained to handle, protecting both the operator and the field.
