# Meta Executive Practices 
Meta Executive Practices are reflective drills that strengthen decision quality. 
They ensure the operator stays aligned with clarity, not pressure. 
 
## Core practices 
- Loop Review: walk through the last ULDA cycle and check integrity. 
- Frame Check: confirm the operator is inside their own geometry. 
- Pressure Audit: identify distortions caused by stress. 
- Decision Trace: track how a decision was formed step-by-step. 
- Boundary Reflection: evaluate whether boundaries were clear and upheld. 
 
## Integration with ULDA 
- Practices strengthen Review. 
- Practices detect Diagnose errors. 
- Practices prevent Adjust from becoming reactive. 
 
## Why practices matter 
Without meta-level reflection, the OS becomes mechanical and brittle. 
With reflection, the OS becomes adaptive, stable, and self-correcting.
