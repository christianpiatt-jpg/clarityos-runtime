============================================================
Langbridg Binding — Professional Mediation Protocol
============================================================

NAME:
Langbridg Language Layer

VERSION:
1.0

FUNCTION:
Convert protocol outputs into clean, human, neutral professional
language without altering structure or meaning.

POSITION IN STACK:
PM-IP     (Pressure Addressing)
PM-TCP    (Clarity Transport)
PM-APP    (Application Layer)
Langbridg (Language Layer)
Surface   (Website, Documents, Interfaces)

RULES:
1. Preserve meaning exactly.
2. Remove adverbs and intensifiers.
3. Remove jargon and technical drift.
4. Maintain cultural neutrality.
5. Maintain emotional safety.
6. Use minimal, direct sentences.
7. Keep tone steady, human, and professional.
8. Do not modify protocol logic or structure.

INPUT:
Any output from PM-IP, PM-TCP, or PM-APP.

OUTPUT:
A human-readable version of the same content.

FAILURE MODE:
If language becomes unclear or distorted, revert to protocol output
and re-run Langbridg.

STATUS:
Bound and Active.
