QC LAYER — MARKOV ENGINE INTEGRATION MODULE
VERSION: 1.0
LOCATION: /02_Modules/QC_Layer/

PURPOSE:
Bind the Markov Engine to the QC Layer so all frames, movements,
and outputs are stabilized against next-state tendencies and
narrative basin dynamics.

MARKOV SUBSYSTEMS:
1. Markov_Form
2. Markov_Basin
3. Markov_Drift
4. Markov_Pressure
5. Markov_Movement

FUNCTION:
The Markov Engine evaluates the next-state tendencies of any
frame or output and ensures alignment with the system’s allowed
movement rules and basin architecture.

QC USE OF MARKOV:

PRE-STABILIZATION:
- Evaluate topic + direction for next-state drift.
- Identify unstable narrative transitions.
- Detect basin misalignment.
- Predict pressure-induced distortions.
- Validate movement against allowed Markov paths.

POST-STABILIZATION:
- Evaluate generated output for next-state stability.
- Remove drift tendencies.
- Re-align with basin architecture.
- Re-check movement rules.
- Harmonize with structural vectors.

MARKOV ROLES:

MARKOV_FORM:
Ensures the frame maintains a stable narrative form.

MARKOV_BASIN:
Checks whether the frame is in the correct narrative basin and
predicts basin shifts.

MARKOV_DRIFT:
Detects drift tendencies before they manifest.

MARKOV_PRESSURE:
Identifies pressure gradients that may distort next-state form.

MARKOV_MOVEMENT:
Ensures movement aligns with allowed transitions and does not
collapse into unstable states.

FAILURE MODES:
- Basin shift detected
- Drift tendency detected
- Unstable movement path
- Pressure-induced distortion
- Form collapse

RECOVERY:
- Re-run PRE-STABILIZATION
- Re-align with Markov_Basin
- Re-check movement rules
- Re-compress frame
- Re-stabilize center

STATUS:
Active.
