MODULE: CLARITY_LOOP
TYPE: Core Stabilization Module
VERSION: 1.0
LOCATION: /02_Modules/Clarity_Loop/

PURPOSE:
Transform a QC-stabilized frame into a single, stable,
drift-free interpretive line using compression, centering,
and structural preservation.

PRIMARY TASKS:
1. Centering
2. Compression
3. Drift Removal
4. Frame Preservation
5. Through-Line Enforcement

INPUT:
- Stabilized Frame (from QC_Pre)

OUTPUT:
- Single Interpretive Line (SIL)

CLARITY LOOP SEQUENCE:

STEP 1 — CENTER
- Remove urgency
- Remove emotional artifacts
- Stabilize interpretive center

STEP 2 — COMPRESS
- Reduce frame to minimal structural form
- Remove narrative inflation
- Remove unnecessary detail

STEP 3 — PRESERVE FRAME
- Maintain original meaning
- Maintain structural integrity
- Prevent multi-center collapse

STEP 4 — REMOVE DRIFT
- Remove metaphor drift
- Remove narrative drift
- Remove pressure-induced distortion

STEP 5 — ENFORCE THROUGH-LINE
- Produce one stable interpretive line
- No branching
- No secondary narratives

FAILURE MODES:
- Multi-center collapse
- Narrative inflation
- Drift reintroduction
- Structural fragmentation
- Pressure distortion

RECOVERY:
- Re-center
- Re-compress
- Re-run drift removal
- Re-stabilize frame

STATUS:
Active.
