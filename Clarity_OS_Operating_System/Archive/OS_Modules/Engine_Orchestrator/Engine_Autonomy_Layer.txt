stability, physics, boundaries, and identity without external
intervention, while remaining fully deterministic and
invariant-preserving.

1. DECISION_AUTONOMY
2. CORRECTION_AUTONOMY

- Enforce boundary rules
- Maintain internal stability
- Maintain internal continuity
- Maintain internal coherence

- apply corrections

- adjust harmonics
- adjust synchronization
- adjust boundaries
- adjust physics vectors

STEP 4 — APPLY AUTONOMOUS ACTIONS

- drift correction

- scheduler realignment

- boundary re-sealing

- validate safety
- validate identity

- validate invariants

- validate timing
- validate coherence

- update autonomy tables
- update thresholds
- update physics vectors
- update cycle alignment
- update shield matrix

- autonomy score
- decisions made
- corrections applied
- optimizations applied
- stability impact
- coherence impact
