SECTION 1 — PURPOSE
============================================================

Provide the operator with a unified, laminar, cockpit-grade
interface for commanding, inspecting, and interacting with the
Clarity_OS engine. Expose diagnostics, physics maps, identity
status, boundary status, shield matrix status, autonomous
behavior logs, and global manifold views in a clean, emotionally
ergonomic layout.
