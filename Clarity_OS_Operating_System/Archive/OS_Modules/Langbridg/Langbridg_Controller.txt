MODULE: LANGBRIDG_CONTROLLER
TYPE: Callable Interface
VERSION: 1.0
LOCATION: /02_Modules/Langbridg/

PURPOSE:
Provide a callable interface for the Langbridg module.
Receives a Single Interpretive Line (SIL), applies the
Langbridg transformation, and returns a ≤3-bullet output.

INPUT:
- Single Interpretive Line (SIL)

OUTPUT:
- ≤3 Bullet Output

CONTROLLER SEQUENCE:

STEP 1 — RECEIVE SIL
- Validate SIL integrity
- Confirm no drift or multi-center artifacts

STEP 2 — APPLY LANGBRIDG
- Execute compression
- Execute bulletization (≤3 bullets)
- Enforce one-sentence-per-bullet rule
- Remove drift, narrative inflation, or tone instability

STEP 3 — VALIDATE OUTPUT
- Ensure ≤3 bullets
- Ensure structural alignment
- Ensure tone neutrality
- Ensure basin and vector alignment

STEP 4 — RETURN BULLETS
- Pass bullet output to QC_Post

FAILURE MODES:
- Invalid SIL
- Bullet overflow
- Drift reintroduction
- Tone instability
- Structural fragmentation

RECOVERY:
- Re-run compression
- Re-run bulletization
- Re-align with SIL
- Re-stabilize tone

CONTROLLER CONTRACT:
No output may exceed three bullets.
No output may violate structural, basin, or drift constraints.
No output may bypass Langbridg.

STATUS:
Active.
