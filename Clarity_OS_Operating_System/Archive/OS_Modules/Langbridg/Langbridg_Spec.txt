MODULE: LANGBRIDG
TYPE: Language Transformation Module
VERSION: 1.2
LOCATION: /02_Modules/Langbridg/

PURPOSE:
Transform a Single Interpretive Line (SIL) into a clean,
structural, ≤3-bullet output suitable for public-facing
communication while preserving frame integrity.

PRIMARY TASKS:
1. Bulletization (≤3 bullets)
2. Drift Removal
3. Structural Preservation
4. Tone Stabilization
5. Compression

INPUT:
- Single Interpretive Line (SIL) from Clarity Loop

OUTPUT:
- ≤3 Bullet Output (one sentence per bullet)

LANGBRIDG RULES:
- Maximum of three bullets
- Minimum of one bullet
- One sentence per bullet
- No adverbs or intensifiers
- No narrative or commentary
- No metaphors or jargon
- Cultural neutrality required
- Tone must remain steady, human, and professional

BULLET LOGIC:
1. Core fact
2. Structural insight
3. Forward-looking clarity (optional)

FAILURE MODES:
- More than three bullets
- Multi-sentence bullets
- Drift or narrative inflation
- Tone instability
- Structural misalignment

RECOVERY:
- Re-run compression
- Re-run drift removal
- Re-align with SIL
- Re-stabilize tone

STATUS:
Active.
