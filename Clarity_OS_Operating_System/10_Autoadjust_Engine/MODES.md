# Autoadjust Modes 
The Autoadjust Engine supports three modes of sensitivity. 
 
## Soft Mode 
- Minimal intervention. 
- Only corrects major drift or collapse. 
 
## Moderate Mode 
- Balanced intervention. 
- Corrects drift, pressure, and loop skipping. 
 
## Aggressive Mode 
- High sensitivity. 
- Detects micro-drift, micro-pressure, and subtle frame collapse. 
- Suggests altitude shifts frequently.
